FiDDL (Face iD Door Lock)

Currently In Development

(Deployment estimated to be fixed by Jan. 25)

This is a Personal Proposal senior design project, Flask web app that allows users to register an account and upload photos. These photos are stored in Google Firebase and used with OpenCV facial recognition to allow hands-free user access to their home using Google Nest Doorbell and Nuki Smart Lock API communication.

Final Grade Calculator APP

(Developed 2019)

Final Grade Calculator App developed with Android Studio to calculate what grade a user needs to get on a Final Exam to get a user-specified Final Grade in the class. This app takes your desired grade (e.g. B), your classes percentage equivalent to that letter (e.g. 83 is usually a B), your current grade in the class, and how much weight your final exam holds (e.g. 25 percent). In the end you are told the score you need to get on your final exam to achieve your desired letter grade!
